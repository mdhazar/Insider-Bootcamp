    • Star Wars: Andor

    Star Wars: Andor follows the story of Cassian Andor during the rise of the Galactic Empire. The series explores his journey from a rogue rebel to a key player in the fight against the Empire.

    Main Characters

    Cassian Andor

    A skilled rebel spy who becomes a crucial figure in the fight against the Empire.

    Mon Mothma

    A key political leader in the Rebellion against the Empire.

    Luthen Rael

    A mysterious figure who plays a key role in organizing the rebellion.

    Episodes

    Episode 1: Kassa

    Cassian Andor looks for his missing sister on the industrial planet of Morlana One, his reckless search for answers about his past makes him a wanted man.

    Episode 2: That Would Be Me

    Cassian attempts to lay low on Ferrix as agents of the law close in.

    Episode 3: Reckoning

    Cassian's desperation to avoid arrest leads him to a mysterious man with unknown connections.

    Episode 4: Aldhani

    Out of options, Cassian is recruited for a dangerous mission to infiltrate an Imperial garrison.

    Episode 5: The Axe Forgets

    Cassian must carefully navigate the distrust inherent in being the new member of a secret operation.
